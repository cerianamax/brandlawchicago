When a construction project goes sideways with delayed payments, defective work, or contract disputes, you need an attorney who understands the industry, not just the law. What You Should Know About Todd R. Brand

Meet Todd Brand

He Has Deep Industry Insight

Construction law demands more than general litigation experience. It requires fluency in the technical side of how projects are conceived, managed, and built. Todd understands the systems behind construction disputes and the realities of project delivery, scheduling, change management, cost controls, and the chain of contracts connecting owners, contractors, subcontractors, and design professionals. That depth of understanding allows Todd to identify the true drivers of a dispute and develop strategies grounded in how construction actually works.

He Cares About Outcomes, Not Appearances

This firm was created with purpose, not pretense. Todd founded the practice on the belief that a client's interests should always come before the firm's. There are no layers of attorneys or paralegals, no marble lobbies, and no unnecessary overhead. If you are looking for a large firm built around appearances rather than outcomes, this may not be the right fit. Todd's focus is, and always will be, efficient strategy, clear communication, and results that matter. His energy is spent solving client problems, not maintaining an image.

He Provides Individual, Focused Attention

Focused, personalized representation is the core of the practice. Every matter is handled personally by Todd. It is never delegated, diluted, or lost in a system of multiple attorneys. From the initial consultation through final resolution, clients work directly with Todd and have consistent access to him throughout the process, including his personal cell phone number. This level of involvement ensures clear communication and allows cases to move efficiently, without the delays or hand-offs common at larger firms. Clients choose Todd because they want an attorney they can reach, rely on, and trust. That is exactly what he provides.

He Believes in Transparency â€“ to a Fault

Todd tells his clients the hard truths, whether good, bad, or somewhere in between, because they deserve straight answers. His approach is rooted in honesty, practicality, and real-world experience. He is not interested in impressing anyone with legal jargon. His focus is on getting things done, protecting what clients have built, and helping them make informed decisions. Clients receive clear explanations, realistic expectations, and consistent communication. Todd earns trust by being upfront, reliable, and focused on solutions that actually work.

He is Determined and Passionate

Many firms measure success by hours billed or files closed. When a lawyer treats a case as just another matter in a tall stack, it inevitably receives only a fraction of the attention it deserves. Construction disputes demand far more than that.

Todd answers to his clients, not partners, billable quotas, or firm politics. Every case receives his full attention, preparation, and determination. He brings strategy, focus, and genuine personal investment to every matter. He works to ensure that each client's story, work, and rights are fully understood and forcefully represented. When Todd takes on a case, he is all in.
